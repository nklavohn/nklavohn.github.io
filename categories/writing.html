A Quick Note

This page is far from a complete collection of my writing, but I wanted to offer a couple snippets of my work as a way of making my portfolio a better representation of my whole self. I have lots of poems and a couple short stories I'd like to publish in an anthology at some point as well, which are not included here.

I handmake my own journals with high-quality leather and Strathmore 300 sketch paper. From March 2019 - December 2020, I wrote 494 pages in these journals, which I estimate to be approximately 250,000 words based on a 500 word-per-page average. My favorite pens are the Nemosine Fission and Kaweco Supra Brass with F nibs, and my favorite ink is Pilot Iroshizuku ku-jaku (a wonderful torquoise).

Stagnancy - A novel ( project_status= IN_PROGRESS ):

This story is an actuary's philosophical reflection on his lonely life in a world operated with thinking caps. Through this lens, he attempts to rationalize tedium, routine, consciousness, meaning, happiness, and all that other tough stuff we don't know the answer to.
