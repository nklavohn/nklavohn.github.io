Methanol Synthesis Plant Design COMPLETE

Methanol is an attractive fuel alternative due to its energy density and the option for carbon neutral manufacturing.

This project was first: optimization of a plant designed by Shi et al. to account for realistic feed and reactor conditions, second: heat integration for energy savings, and third: economic analyses to determine profitability of the plant.
